Creamy Maggie

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 small capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pan, add Maggi noodles
  2. In another pan, melt butter and sauté veggies until soft.
  3. Add milk to the sautéed vegetables
  4. Add the Maggi tastemaker and mix well.
  5. Stir in grated cheese, black pepper, and salt
  6. Serve hot, garnished with extra cheese if desired
