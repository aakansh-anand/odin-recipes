Veg Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 small capsicum (bell pepper), finely chopped
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 small carrot, grated
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare with finely chopped veggies
  2. Thinly slice cucumber and grate the carrot
  3. Mix the Ingredients and masala
  4. Prepare the Bread slices with chutney
  5. Assemble the Sandwich with butter
  6. Add the mixture & cucumber on top of mixture
  7. Add the last slice of butter side
  8. Grill the Sandwich on pan until golden brown
